# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who wants to contribute to VUTOOLS.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct that could be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
